Pi-Hole

I was interested in the Pi-Hole project because it allows you to turn the RaspberryPi into it's own DNS Server and acting as a sort of middle man standing between my devices and the internet. Once ads reach the pi-hole, it gets blacklisted essentially blocking the advertisement.

With such a device one can see all types of content whether it be Videos, mobile games, or music guaranteeing an almost surefire way to use your everyday devices without the need to be bothered by pesky advertisements
