Kodi

The Kodi project is one that focuses on all types of Media Entertainment. You basically have a Dashboard(Homepage) that shows you all kinds of TV shows, movies, and much more from around the world.

Only catch is that you must download them from repositories found online, that streams the content you want to watch via Torrent links. Using a VPN is advisable for some content. And because of portability purposes, it is ideal for the RaspberryPi.
